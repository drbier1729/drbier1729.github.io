Madness and Mayhem!

In Angry Al you will race and blast your way to the finish line as you attempt to survive a gauntlet of enemy gunners and racers!

S0 Engine

This game demo was created in a prototype of the S0 Engine framework (pronounced "S Naught"). The engine framework features an ECS to handle game logic and data, JSON serialization, and more.

Built in C++ with SDL2 and OpenGL

SDL2 and OpenGL allow the S0 Engine to support things like local multiplayer, perspective rendering, custom shaders, and texture loading.
